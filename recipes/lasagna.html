Lasagna

Easy and delicious home made lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (400g)
  • One big onion
  • Ready tomato sauce
  • Ready white sauce
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, herbs)
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Shredded cheese

How to make

  1. Cook ground beef and chopped onions in a pan
  2. Add red sauce and seasonings to the pan
  3. Let it simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Add first layer of lasagna pasta to the casserole, add white sauce and red sauce on top. repeat until casserole is full
  5. Add shredded cheese on top layer
  6. Bake in 175 celsius for 45 minutes
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before eating
