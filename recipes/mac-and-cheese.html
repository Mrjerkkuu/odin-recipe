Mac and Cheese

Delicious and fast mac and cheese for dinner

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni as descripted on the package
  2. Melt butter and add you flour for the cheese sauce
  3. Add your milk to the pot, after milk is heated add you cheese
  4. Add your macaroni to the pot with the sauce alongside with the seasoning
  5. Serve hot
